Necromancer

Necromancers are beings that can live in and out of death while maintaining a physical living state. Many of the other abilities for a necromancer are: spell-casting, communicating, raising the dead, demonology, and other things of that nature.

Player 1 Choose between Necromancer and Healer

Healer

Certain effects used against enemies grant souls to the Healer. Then the Healer can consume souls to create powerful healing effects. It takes a turn or two to start getting souls, but once they have them, the healing effects are very powerful.

Warrior

Warrior character's skills are centered around strength and physical combat with a wide variety of melee weapons, and the ability to survive while tanking for the group, usually at the cost of agility or range.

Player 2 Choose between Warrior and Archer

Archer

Archer is peerless in ranged combat, she can fluidly shift between combat styles to dispense with enemies near and far with deadly grace and efficiency.
